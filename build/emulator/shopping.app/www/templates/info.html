About Chinna Chinna Cinema

Cinna Chinna Cinema - Movie Theater in your pocket ,The Official App from Puthu Yugam TV

The South Indian Television Program application features the short films. Idea is to encourage Tamil Short Film makers.

App contains best of Tamil Short Films which Aired in Puthu Yugam Television every Sunday (at 10:00 AM) -Chinna Chinna Cinema Program.

More movies will be added every week.

Movie Theater in your pocket

App Contains,

  • Tamil Short Films
  • A short Description
  • Notifies when a New Film added.

Simple and entertaining App for all movie lovers.
